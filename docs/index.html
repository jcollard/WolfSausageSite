Wolf Sausage

In the remote forests of a small town, a group of friends embarks on a weekend camping trip to escape the stresses of everyday life. But their idyllic getaway turns into a nightmare when they stumble upon a strange meat market in the middle of nowhere.

As they investigate, they discover that the meat being sold is not from any animal they recognize. Desperate for food, they buy a pack of sausages and cook them over the campfire. But as they eat, they begin to experience strange and terrifying hallucinations.

Soon, the friends realize that they have unknowingly ingested the meat of a pack of wolves that were cursed by a local witch. Now, they must fight for survival as they are hunted by the vengeful and bloodthirsty wolves, who have taken on the form of human-like creatures with a taste for human flesh.

Will the friends be able to make it out alive, or will they become the next victims of the cursed wolf sausage? Find out in this heart-stopping horror film that will leave you questioning what's really in your food."
